# Audio attribution

`adhan.ogg` — audio extracted from “Adhan, Great Mosque of Mecca —
Jan 21, 2013” by Seyfula Islam, from Wikimedia Commons.

`takbir.ogg` — a 7.35-second excerpt from the beginning of the same recording,
containing the opening takbir. A short fade-out was added.

The first 7.8 seconds of silence were removed and the audio was converted
from the original WebM file to Ogg Vorbis.
