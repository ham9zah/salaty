## Adhan audio

The bundled `sounds/adhan.ogg` is extracted from “Adhan, Great Mosque of
Mecca — Jan 21, 2013” by Seyfula Islam. The first 7.8 seconds of silence
were removed, and the audio was converted from WebM to Ogg Vorbis.

The bundled `sounds/takbir.ogg` is a 7.35-second excerpt from the beginning
of the same recording, with a short fade-out.
